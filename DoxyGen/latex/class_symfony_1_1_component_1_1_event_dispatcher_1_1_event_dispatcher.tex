\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
The \mbox{\hyperlink{interface_symfony_1_1_component_1_1_event_dispatcher_1_1_event_dispatcher_interface}{Event\+Dispatcher\+Interface}} is the central point of Symfony\textquotesingle{}s event listener system.

Listeners are registered on the manager and events are dispatched through the manager.
